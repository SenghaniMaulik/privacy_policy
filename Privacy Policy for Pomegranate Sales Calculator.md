**Last Updated:** February 14, 2026

**Developer:** Maulik Senghani

## Introduction

This privacy policy applies to the Pomegranate Sales Calculator mobile application (the "App"). This policy describes how we handle information in our App.

---

## Information Collection and Use

### Data Stored Locally

The App stores the following information **ONLY on your device**:

- Farmer names
- Mandi/Market names
- Buyer/Trader names
- Vehicle numbers
- Transaction notes
- Weight and price calculations
- Sales history
- Calculation results
- App settings (theme, language preferences)
- Custom calculation values (grading expense, loading expense, etc.)

**Important:** All this data is stored ONLY on your device. We do NOT collect, transmit, or have access to any of this information.

### Data We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect:
- Personal identification information
- Location data
- Contact information
- Financial information
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics
- Advertising data
- Any other personal data

---

## Data Storage

- All data is stored locally on your Android device
- Data is saved in the app's private storage area
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No data is shared with third parties
- No cloud backup or sync

---

## Data Control

You have full control over your data:

- **View:** Access all your saved transactions in the History section
- **Delete:** Remove individual transactions or clear all history
- **Export:** Generate PDF receipts of your transactions
- **Uninstall:** Deleting the app will remove all stored data permanently

---

## Third-Party Services

This App does NOT use:
- Analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
- Advertising services
- Social media integrations
- Cloud storage services
- Any third-party data collection tools

---

## Permissions

The App requests the following Android permissions:

### WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
- **Purpose:** To save PDF receipts to your device
- **Usage:** Only when you choose to generate and save a PDF receipt
- **Data Access:** Only the PDF files created by the app

### Internet Permission (if applicable)
- **Purpose:** Not currently used
- **Note:** The app works fully offline

---

## Children's Privacy

This App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App is designed for agricultural professionals and business users.

## Data Security

We take data security seriously:

- All data is stored in your device's secure app storage
- No data transmission over the internet
- No unauthorized access to your data
- Data is isolated from other apps on your device
